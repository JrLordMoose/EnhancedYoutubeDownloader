Version 0.4.0: YouTube Shorts Support

Released: October 14, 2025

We're excited to announce full support for YouTube Shorts! Download your favorite short-form content directly using youtube.com/shorts/* URLs.

What's New

  • YouTube Shorts URL Support - Paste any youtube.com/shorts/VIDEO_ID URL and download instantly
  • Seamless Integration - Works with all existing features: subtitles, quality selection, batch operations
  • Format Options - Download Shorts in MP4, WebM, or audio-only formats

Why This Matters

YouTube Shorts have become a major content format. Whether you're a creator archiving your work, a researcher studying trends, or simply want to save your favorite clips, Enhanced YouTube Downloader now handles Shorts as effortlessly as regular videos.

Technical Details

The update includes enhanced regex pattern matching in our QueryResolver service, ensuring reliable detection and processing of Shorts URLs. All existing quality selection, subtitle burning, and metadata features work seamlessly with Shorts.
